Disc Golf Rules

Learn how to play and understand the basic rules of the game.

Objective

Disc golf is played much like traditional golf, but instead of hitting a ball into a hole, players throw a flying disc into a metal basket with hanging chains. The goal is to complete each hole in the fewest number of throws.

Basic Rules

  1. Starting the Game: Each player begins at the tee area. Order is random on the first hole.
  2. Throwing the Disc: The player farthest from the basket throws next.
  3. Tee Throws: Must be made from within the designated tee area.
  4. Fairway Throws: Your foot must stay directly behind the disc’s lie.
  5. Putting: Within 10 meters, keep your balance and do not step past the lie.
  6. Completion: A hole is finished when the disc lands in the basket or chains.
  7. Out of Bounds: One penalty throw is added.
  8. Scoring: Lowest total number of throws wins.

How to Win

Just like regular golf, the winner is the player with the lowest total score after all 9 holes are completed.
